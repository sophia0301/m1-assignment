The Tuning Squad was started by me, Sophia Tapia and my two brothers Alejandro Garcia, and Noah Tapia. The inspiration came from when my brother got into the car scene. After he started going to meets and being around cars more. It sparked my interest and got me into the car scene. Later on down the road, the Tuning Squad was born. While any normal auto body shop can help in starting off your modification journey, the Tuning Squad brings its own unigue take on modifying. The Tuning Squad schedules appointments and does at home jobs bringing all the tools and essentials to the customers house and complete everything right there at their home.
